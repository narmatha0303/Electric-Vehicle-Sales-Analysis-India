# 🚗 AtliQ Motors: India EV Market Entry Strategy
### *A Data-Driven Blueprint for Unlocking the World's Most Dynamic EV Market*

## 📌 Project Overview
AtliQ Motors, a US-based automotive leader with a 25% market share in North America, is preparing to enter the Indian EV landscape. This project provides a comprehensive market study (2022–2024) to guide Bruce Haryali, Head of AtliQ Motors India, in executing a successful entry strategy.

**Workflow Strategy:** `DETECT` Market Gaps | `ANALYZE` Penetration Trends | `AUTOMATE` Sales Intelligence

## 🎯 Objective & Scope
- **Objective:** Conduct a multi-dimensional market study of the Indian EV/Hybrid industry.
- **Methodology:** STAR Method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for analytical rigor.
- **Tech Stack:** SQL, Power BI, Advanced Excel.

---

## 📊 Key Insights (The "Data Guardian" Perspective)
* **High-Growth Segment:** BMW India achieved an explosive **1141% CAGR**, signaling massive potential in the premium segment.
* **Regional Leaders:** Meghalaya leads with the highest state-level CAGR at **28.5%**, followed by Goa.
* **Seasonal Intelligence:** March consistently records peak sales units, reaching **138,343 units** in 2024.
